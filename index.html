About Us

Mana Andhra Dosa brings you the spicy, tangy, and crispy flavors of traditional Andhra-style dosas.

We started with a passion to serve authentic Andhra tastes to everyone, ensuring high quality and affordability.

Our mission is to bring the street-style dosa experience into a clean, vibrant, and welcoming atmosphere where every bite feels like home.
